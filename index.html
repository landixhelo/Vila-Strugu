About Vila Strugu

Vila Strugu is offering accommodation in Voskopojë. Each accommodation at the 3-star hotel has city views, and guests can enjoy access to a garden and to a bar. There is a children's playground and guests can make use of free WiFi and free private parking.

The Vila will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms offering a desk, a kettle, a minibar, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV, a balcony and a private bathroom with a bidet. All guest rooms feature a wardrobe. Speaking Greek, English and Italian, staff are ready to help around the clock at the reception.
